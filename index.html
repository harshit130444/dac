Who We Are
Empowering Students
Through AI

DAC (DGU AI Cell) is the official artificial intelligence student body of DBS Global University, Dehradun. We are a student-driven community dedicated to exploring, learning, and building with AI.

We bring together curious minds from across departments to collaborate on real-world projects, attend expert-led workshops, and participate in national-level competitions — all centered around the transformative power of artificial intelligence.

Our mission: democratize AI education and foster a culture of innovation at DGU.
