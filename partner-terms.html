Partnership Agreement

Partner Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms carefully before registering as a partner of the DAC — DBS AI Cell. Ticking the agreement box and submitting the partnership form constitutes your full acceptance of everything below.

Version 1.0 Academic Year 2025–2026 DBS Global University

Minimum Commitment — One Year Lock-in

Once a partnership is confirmed, it remains in effect for a minimum term of one (1) year from the date of confirmation. Partners may not withdraw, cancel, or dissolve the partnership before completing this 12-month term, except with prior written approval from the DAC leadership and Faculty Advisor. Registering signifies your acceptance of this commitment.

01

One-Year Minimum Commitment

A confirmed partnership carries a non-withdrawable minimum term of one year. Early exit is only permitted where DAC leadership and the Faculty Advisor agree to it in writing, or where DAC itself terminates the partnership for cause as described in these terms.

02

Accuracy of Information

All information provided in the partnership registration form must be accurate, current, and complete. DAC may verify submitted details and reserves the right to decline, pause, or revoke any partnership founded on false, misleading, or incomplete information.

03

Data Collection & Contact Consent

By submitting the form, you consent to DAC storing your details in its official records and spreadsheets, and to being contacted by DAC regarding partnership coordination, events, opportunities, and related updates. Your data will be used only for legitimate partnership purposes.

04

Scope of Partnership

Registration enables collaboration on mutually agreed activities such as advisory sessions, events, live projects, mentorship, internships, or sponsorship. It does not by itself create any employment, agency, or binding commercial contract unless a separate written agreement is signed by both parties.

05

Use of Name, Logo & Branding

Partners grant DAC permission to acknowledge the partnership using their name and logo on the DAC website, brochures, and event material. Neither party may misrepresent the nature or extent of the partnership. Branding will be removed on request once the partnership formally concludes.

06

Responsible & Ethical AI

All joint activities must align with DAC's AI Ethics & Responsible AI Framework. Partners agree not to engage the Cell or its members in any deceptive, discriminatory, privacy-violating, surveillance-based, or otherwise harmful use of AI.

07

Confidentiality

Any non-public information exchanged during the partnership — including student data, internal plans, or proprietary material — must be kept confidential and used solely for the agreed collaboration. This obligation continues beyond the end of the partnership.

08

No Automatic Financial Obligation

Registering as a partner creates no automatic financial obligation for either party. Any sponsorship, funding, paid engagement, or resource commitment must be documented in a separate written agreement before it becomes binding.

09

Student Welfare & Fair Engagement

Partners engaging students through internships or projects must do so ethically and fairly — with a clear learning scope, safe and respectful conditions, no exploitative unpaid labour beyond what is agreed, and appropriate credit or certification where applicable.

10

Code of Conduct & Termination for Cause

Partners and their representatives must act professionally and respectfully at all times. Harassment, discrimination, or conduct that brings disrepute to DGU or DAC may result in immediate termination of the partnership by DAC, notwithstanding the one-year term.

11

Amendments & Governing Terms

These terms may be updated from time to time; the version in effect at the time of your registration governs your partnership. Where a separate signed agreement exists, that agreement prevails over these terms. All matters are subject to DGU institutional policy and the jurisdiction of Dehradun, Uttarakhand.

By ticking the agreement checkbox and submitting the partnership registration form, you confirm that you have read, understood, and accepted all of the terms above, including the one-year minimum commitment. For any questions, write to dac@dgu.ac.in.
